Tofu Scramble

This recipe will tell you how to make a vegetarian tofu scramble dish but with an indian twist that will leave you wanting leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 1 package extra firm tofu
  • 1 medium tomato
  • 1 medium onion
  • 5 mushrooms
  • 1 green pepper
  • paneer masala
  • red chili powder
  • tumeric

NOTE the spices will be to taste and personal preference

Directions

  1. Squeeze out all water from extra firm tofu and make it into a crumble. The smaller the pieces the better.
  2. Marinate tofu in all spices. Add more spice than you think is necessary due to the bland nature of tofu
  3. Sautee veggies in pan until just before tender
  4. Add the spiced tofu mixture to pan and cook until tofu is golden yellow
  5. Add salt to taste
