Artichoke Dip

This recipe will make you the hit of the party. guaranteed!

The following recipe has been inspired by Allrecipes' artchoke dip recipe. I have made my own additions to this recipe to make it a little more of my own. However, the base recipe on their website is more than good enough and I highly recommend it as well. This recipe calls for a hot dish version of Spinach artichoke dip. This works well as an appetizer or side dish. Just pop it into the oven until golden brown and serve. Serving with crackers or chips or veggies is good but I think my personal favourite is using naan dippers to really get your scoop on and get as much of this wonderful dip as you can with each bite. This dish is going to go in a hurry

Ingredients

  • 1 (8oz) package cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup sour cream 5%
  • 1/2 cup Marble cheese
  • 1 to 2 cloves of garlic (depending on taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 can artichoke hearts, chopped
  • 1 1/2 cup of fresh spinach, chopped

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and gather all the ingredients. Lightly grease a baking dish
  2. Sautee the fresh spinach with the minced garlic until spinach has wilted
  3. Mix together the cream cheese and sour cream in the baking dish. Mix together well, until blended smooth together. Add the spices to the mixture. Note: softening the cream cheese at room temperature helps mixing
  4. Stir in the drained and chopped artichoke hearts and the cooked spinach into the mixture.
  5. Top with cheese.
  6. Bake in the oven until bubbly and lightly browned, approx 20 mins

Leftovers can be stored in the fridge and reheated as needed. If you are not serving right away after making the dish, store in the fridge until you are ready to put into the oven.
